Cajun Chicken Burger

Once grilled to juicy perfection, the Cajun-spiced chicken breasts are crowned with a velvety slice of melted pepper jack cheese, adding a creamy richness that perfectly complements the intense heat of the seasoning. But the real star of the show? A generous slathering of zesty remoulade sauce, infused with tangy mustard, piquant horseradish, and a hint of lemon, creating a symphony of flavors that dance on the palate.
